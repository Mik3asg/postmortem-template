# 📋 Postmortem Template

A clean, professional postmortem template for DevOps and SRE teams — inspired by the [Google SRE Workbook, Chapter 10](https://sre.google/workbook/postmortem-culture/).

---

## What's Inside

A ready-to-use `.docx` template covering the full structure of a blameless postmortem:

- Executive Summary
- Detection & Impact
- Root Cause (with 5 Whys guidance)
- Timeline
- Resolution
- Lessons Learned (What went well / poorly / where we got lucky)
- Action Items — five themed tables with Type, Priority, Owner, and Tracking Bug
- Glossary

Designed to work for any incident — from a single-team SSL outage to a multi-region infrastructure failure.

---

## How to Use

1. Download `postmortem-template-devops-sre.docx`
2. Fill in the placeholders (shown in *italic grey*)
3. Delete any sections not relevant to your incident
4. Share with your team and track action items in your ticketing system

---

## Who Is This For

Anyone working in DevOps, SRE, or infrastructure who wants a structured, repeatable way to document incidents and drive improvement. No fluff, no corporate bloat — just the sections that matter.
